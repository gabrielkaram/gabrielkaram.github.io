Getchell Brothers, Inc.

In the summers, I work at Getchell Brothers, Inc., an ice company located in Brewer, Maine. Their Maine stations consist of a Brewer and a Sanford location. They actually just recently expanded some of their production into Bangor, due to a recent explosion in summertime demand for ice. The company has served New England since 1888. In Maine, they have a virtual monopoly of the ice business, and have branched into ice cream supplying as well.
